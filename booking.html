A warm welcome—complete with a lobby fireplace—awaits you at San Marcos. Located in Mexico, our contemporary hotel is accessible to a host of outdoor recreation options, downtown cultural attractions, and global business headquarters. We are pleased to offer our guests large, well-designed rooms and a host of sought-after amenities. Enjoy free Wi-Fi, climate-controlled rooms with 50-inch flat-screen TVs, and seating areas and steam showers in select rooms and suites.

The property also features three generous meeting spaces totaling 3,700 sq. ft. (343.7 sq. m) for your next business or personal event. Enjoy our indoor heated pool and hot tub, or grab an energizing workout at our on-site fitness centre—all open 24 hours. Freeport Food & Drink restaurant and lounge is located on site for convenient, delicious breakfast and dinner daily.
